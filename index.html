Influence and Trajectory

Throughout my career journey, I've authored impactful papers, filed innovative patents, mentored students, managed an R&D team, and coordinated several groundbreaking projects.

  • 2004–2007: BSc in Mathematical Physics, University of Nottingham
  • 2007–2011: PhD in Applied Mathematics, University of Bristol
  • 2011–2012: Research Fellow, Max Planck Institute (MPI-PKS), Dresden
  • 2012–2017: Senior Research Engineer, Toshiba Research Laboratory
  • 2017–2019: Joined Ultraleap / Directed R&D & Academic Program
  • 2019–2021: Marie Curie Fellowship & further projects
  • 2021–Present: Head of R&D Partnerships at Ultraleap & Funding Lead at AcoustoFab
